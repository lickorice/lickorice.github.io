A **sweat** is gaming slang for "an extremely or excessively competitive player." The plural form of sweat is **sweats**.

> A: "I don't play Mobile Legends anymore because of the **sweats**."
> B: "Yeah, no one plays for fun anymore!"

In a gaming context, its **antonym** would be **casual**. Oftentimes, prepended with "filthy" (derogatory, though jokingly).
