A **1.0 release** (one-point-oh) is typically done by games with an extended [[early access]] period. It is called so since games in development (among almost every other criterion of software) are [versioned](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Software_versioning) as **0.X.Y.Z**, where **1.0** signifies that it's "ready."
