A **Stand user** is a character in [[JJBA|JoJo's Bizarre Adventure]] (JJBA) known for controlling "**Stands**": a manifestation of a person's "spirit" which possess certain *special powers* (not unlike superheroes/villains). They were introduced in JJBA Part 3, and are a mainstay of the series moving forward. Nearly all main protagonists, and main and side antagonists of the series are Stand users, and they're known for dressing *quite conspicuously,* despite the need for being "undercover" (as Stand users are often sought out and eliminated)

**Stands** are also typically named after Western bands, albums, or songs.
