**Emetophobia** is the phobia related to **vomit**. I have it. It's probably the *only* significant phobia I have.

## What I hate, in order:
- **Me throwing up**. That's the worst case scenario. So far, I've probably only threw up ~10 times in my life.
	- Last one was in 2024, when I *greened out* in Bangkok.
	- Before that, was in 2020, during a Discord call with friends on my birthday. A bottle of "classic" soju was involved.
- Seeing or hearing other people throwing up, or about to throw up, physically.
	- E.g., during my Batanes trip, I had to be with ~50-100 people in a boat in relatively rough waters. I personally have zero problems with the voyage, but being surrounded with 50 other people throwing up was hell for me. I had to completely block *any* perception—menthol inhalers in my nostrils, earbuds blasting music, eyes to the open sea.
- The smell of vomit.
- Seeing other people throw up, online.

## What's OK
- Talking about it. I wrote this article without any issue.
