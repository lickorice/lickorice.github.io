**Tone indicators** help convey the tone of a message, typically in online text discussions. I personally use them most commonly when I would like to convey sarcasm (as the limitations of text greatly restricts the conveyance of sarcasm), with `/s` (pronounced "slash S"). See the Wikipedia link above for more examples.

Personal usage, in order of frequency:
- `/s`. **Sarcasm**.
