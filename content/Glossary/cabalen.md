**Cabalen** (alternatively, **kabalen**), is what a [[kapampangan-people|Kapampangan]] calls another, fellow Kapampangan. It's also a name of a [popular restaurant chain](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cabalen) in the Philippines.

## Etymology

From ka- + balen (which means "town", or "homeland"). 

Similar to the Tagalog kabayan / kababayan.
