# Difficulty

Definitely had some trouble with this one—similar to the first fight with Lace in Deep Docks. Was attempting to see which attacks are parryable or not + the optimal reaction per attack. However, despite expecting to lose in the run above, I saw that Lace was in their third phase and just decided to use tools and finish the fight there. I still have some notes below re: moves to look out for. Personally took me around **~10 tries**.

The **[[runback]]** takes a little bit of time (waiting in the elevator) but remains rather trivial. Ensure you're resting at the bench **below the elevator, within Cog Core**.

- **Magnetite Dice**. Saves you *sometimes*. You'll see it proc in the recording above.

# Tips

1. Lace is **small** and is harder to hit. Ensure your attacks are precise.
2. I've found only a few moves that you can consistently parry:
	1. Lace's downward attack typically places Lace behind Hornet, gives you a free hit afterward.
	2. Lace's upward attack leading to said downward attack is also pogo-able.
3. Do **NOT** attempt to parry the dash, unless you know what you're doing. Lace can stop the dash intersecting their hitbox with Hornet, dealing contact damage.
4. Likewise, do not attempt to parry both ripostes. Best way to deal with them is dash backward immediately (which evades both ripostes, on the ground and in the air), or jump *beyond* Lace and do whatever you want. **Do NOT** jump backward as this puts you in danger of the aerial riposte.
5. I've been personally unsuccessful with parrying the triple hit attack, and this may also lead to contact damage with Lace's forward-moving hitbox. Best way to deal with this is to dash backwards (jumping upwards may be unreliable, unless you have the space).
