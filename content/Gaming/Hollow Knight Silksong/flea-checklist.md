> [!info] This page is long! 
> Use the table of contents on the right side of this page to navigate easily

> [!success] This page uses `localStorage` to store your checklist!
> No need to log-in or anything. Personally made this page to keep track of my own progress to get to Act 3. To test, check an item and reload the page—the state of the checkboxes should be persistent.
> 
> Sadly, given the nature of `localStorage`, this wouldn't work across devices.

> [!danger] This page contains heavy content spoilers!
> I personally advise you to look at this page once you've exhausted your own exploration efforts—as finding things out, experiencing the atmosphere of an unexplored area, are things you cannot replicate once spoiled. However, if you want to play your game efficiently (i.e. "I have stuff to do outside gaming"), I hope this page helps you do that. **It's a singleplayer game after all, play how you want to play!**
# References

I used [mapgenie](https://mapgenie.io/hollow-knight-silksong/maps/pharloom) for the map screenshots. I prefer having my completion in an organized list rather than in a map format (plus there may be some monetization from mapgenie themselves) hence this page.

> Each collectible will be shown an image of the map, and then listed from top to bottom (left to right in case of ties).
